Short Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of Helsinki working with Mikko Myrskyla within the FLUX consortium. I was a PhD candidate in Economics between 2018 and 2022 at the University of Groningen (RUG), and I obtained my Research Master degree in Economics from RUG in 2018.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Demographic and family studies

  • Labor economics
