Short Bio

I received the Bachelor degree in Economics from Zhejiang Gongshang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2016. I obtained my Research Master degree in Economics from University of Groningen (RUG) in 2018. Since September 2018, I have been a PhD candidate at RUG, under the supervision of Rob Alessie, Full Professor, and Max Groneck, Associate Professor.
