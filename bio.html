Ziwei's Biography

  • Since September 2018, I have been a PhD candidate at the Department of Economics, Econometrics and Finance (EEF), University of Groningen (RUG), Groningen, the Netherlands, under the supervision of Professor Rob Alessie and Dr. Max Groneck.

  • I obtained my Research Master degree in Economics from University of Groningen, Groningen, the Netherlands, in 2018.

  • I received the Bachelor degree in Economics from Zhejiang Gongshang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2016.

  • I was born in October 1995, in Chuzhou, Anhui, China.
